Gumbo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound andouille sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 quart chicken broth
  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add flour and stir constantly until the mixture turns a caramel color.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  3. Add sausage and chicken. Cook until chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce, and chicken broth. Season with Cajun seasoning, thyme, basil, oregano, cayenne pepper, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour.
  6. Stir in shrimp and cook until pink.
  7. Remove bay leaf and serve gumbo over cooked rice.
