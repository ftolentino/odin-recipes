Fish Tacos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound white fish fillets
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 head cabbage, shredded
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 8 corn tortillas

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill for high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine olive oil, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, paprika, salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper. Mix in fish fillets, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl, mix sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Refrigerate until needed.
  4. Heat grill to high heat. Place fish on grill, and discard marinade. Cook for 4 minutes per side, or until fish is easily flaked with a fork.
  5. Heat the corn tortillas on the grill until toasted. Break up fish into chunks. Serve in tortillas, topped with cabbage, cilantro, green onions, and sauce.
